Menofia University

Welcome to Menofia University, a leading institution of higher education in Egypt. Established in 1976, Menofia University has a long history of academic excellence and is dedicated to providing high-quality education and research opportunities to students from all over the world.

Our faculties and departments offer a wide range of undergraduate and graduate programs in fields such as medicine, engineering, agriculture, business, and humanities.
